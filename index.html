Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vivamax?

Vivamax is a subscription based, video on-demand app that features your favorite blockbuster Pinoy and foreign movies, TV series, documentaries, concerts, stand-up comedies, fitness & workout shows, sports & genre-bending exclusives that are ALL proudly Filipino made.

How can I access Vivamax?

You can access Vivamax through its website or by downloading the Vivamax app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store on your smartphone, tablet, or smart TV.

What kind of content does Vivamax offer?

Vivamax offers a diverse selection of content, including blockbuster movies, classic films, indie gems, TV series, documentaries, and exclusive original content produced by Vivamax.

Is Vivamax available worldwide?

Vivamax is available for streaming worldwide, allowing Filipino viewers around the globe to enjoy their favorite movies and shows anytime, anywhere.

Are there subscription plans available for Vivamax?

Yes, Vivamax offers subscription plans with flexible pricing options to suit different viewing preferences and budgets. Subscribers can choose between monthly, quarterly, or annual plans.

Can I watch content offline on Vivamax?

Yes, Vivamax allows subscribers to download their favorite movies and shows for offline viewing. This feature is particularly useful for users who want to watch content without an internet connection.

Does Vivamax offer ad-supported viewing?

Vivamax primarily operates on a subscription-based model and does not offer ad-supported viewing. However, occasional promotional offers or free trials may be available to new users.
